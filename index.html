About this survey

This app uses official election materials first, then nonpartisan or mainstream reporting for context.

It avoids an automatic “best match” calculation because voting preferences are value judgments and source interpretation can be contested.

How to use this safely

  1. Survey: react to blinded candidate cards, optionally rate sources, then review fit scores.
  2. Candidate list: reveal identities and research candidates more deeply.
  3. About: review this process and reset your saved progress.

Candidate-selection notes

The candidate list defaults to filtering Governor to candidates named at 1% or higher in the newest poll found during the May 4, 2026 web check. Use “Show full official list” to see every ballot-listed candidate preserved from the official materials.

For local San Mateo County offices, the app includes the candidates visible in the county roster for the offices likely associated with this ballot starter. Because one photo cannot fully verify every address-specific contest, the local section is a research scaffold rather than a final ballot reconstruction.
